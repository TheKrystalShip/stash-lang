# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **[Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/)** in full, without modification. The canonical text — including the pledge, standards, enforcement responsibilities, scope, and enforcement guidelines — is published at:

<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

A plain-text rendering is also available at the same URL with `.txt` appended.

## Summary

Participation in the Stash project — issues, pull requests, discussions, the playground, and any other community space affiliated with this repository — is governed by the linked Code of Conduct. By contributing, you agree to abide by it.

The aim is straightforward: be respectful, be patient, and assume good faith. The full document covers the boundaries and the enforcement process in detail.

## Reporting

Conduct concerns can be reported privately to the project maintainer at **cristian.moraru@live.com**, with `[stash-conduct]` in the subject line. Reports are read by the maintainer only.

Please include, where you can:

- Where the incident took place (issue link, PR link, discussion thread, etc.)
- A description of what happened
- Anyone else involved or who witnessed it
- Whether you've taken any action already (replied, blocked, closed)

You will get an acknowledgement within five business days. The maintainer will investigate, decide on a response based on the enforcement guidelines in the linked Covenant, and follow up with you on the outcome.
